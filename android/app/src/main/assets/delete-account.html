Developer: OXIAI / Spark Dating App

⚠️ Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

📱 How to Delete Your Account

1 Open the Spark app on your device
2 Go to the Settings tab (gear icon ⚙️)
3 Scroll down to the Account section
4 Tap on Delete Account
5 Enter your password to confirm
6 Tap Permanently Delete

🗑️ What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile information (name, bio, photos)
  • Your email address and account credentials
  • Your likes and passes history
  • Your matches and chat messages
  • Your blocked users list
  • All personal data associated with your account

📊 What We Keep (for legal/security reasons)

  • Anonymized analytics data (cannot identify you)
  • Transaction records (if applicable, for 7 years for legal compliance)
ℹ️ Note: Data deletion is immediate upon confirmation. There is no additional retention period for personal data. Your account will be permanently removed from our systems within 24 hours.
